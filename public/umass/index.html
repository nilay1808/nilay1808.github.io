My UMass Experience

My name is Nilay Sadavarte and this is an overview of my time here at UMass.

  • The Beginning

    After spending about 18 years in India, I moved to the United States to go to college at UMass! I had never been to UMass, never lived away from home for more than a month. I was a Computer Science major but didn't know a thing about Computer Science!

  • My First Semester

    It was a semester of first for me! Obviously my first semester in college but also my first time experiencing the Fall and the Winter, the first time writing code, getting my first job, and so on.....

  • A very nice end to a successful year

    My first year ended up being a huge success for me. I was sure about my major and what I was doing, I had a very strong and close group of friends, me and my roommate are like best friends! And I was finally travelling back home after being away for 9 months!

  • A year full of responsibilities

    Sophomore year started with a bang, where I was taking a bunch of hard classes and was taking 20 credits, was managing 2 jobs, and was starting a new student org on campus.

  • Finding new purpose

    Starting BUILD UMass was a very eye opening experience and taught me a lot of things about the world, computer science, and myself. It was truly fulfilling to be able to use my skills in my field to make a meaningful impact in the world!

  • Exposure to Industry

    I was lucky enough to get selected for an Internship-like program for Software Engineering at Uber. I spend all of summer and the start of Junior year working on some really cool projects across the organization.

  • Family

    After finishing a very hectic second year, going back and seeing my family was really relaxing. To take a much needed break from life and be able to spend some time with each other, we decided to go on a vacation to Malaysia!

  • UMass Proud!

    Was chosen as one of 10 people on the Homcoming Court to represent UMass. Got to meet and network with a lot of different students and alumni throughout homecoming week and learn about their experiences at UMass.

  • Looking Forward

    Am greatful enough to have an internship lined up at Facebook for the upcoming summer. Also am super excited for BUILD and how rapidly we're growing! 

  • The exciting future!
