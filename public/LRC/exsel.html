ExSEL

ExSEL is the LRC’s group tutoring program. ExSEL provides students with question based academic support in a small group. The content covered during ExSEL sessions is driven by the students in session. ExSEL leaders follow along with what is happening in class and are prepared to answer the questions about content.

ExSEL focuses on the collaborative nature of learning and encourages active participation from attendees. Each ExSEL session is 75 minutes long and are course section specific. Students are able to drop into ExSEL Sessions or call the LRC for an Appointment.
